FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about downloads, quality, and safety.

Is this video downloader really free?

Yes—100% free with no registration. We don’t gate formats behind paywalls and we never require an account.

What platforms are supported?

YouTube, Instagram, and Pinterest. We aim to keep compatibility current; availability may vary if platforms change their endpoints.

Do you support 4K video and MP3 audio?

Yes. If the source provides high-resolution streams, you can save up to 4K MP4. You can also extract high-quality MP3 when available.

Playlists, Reels & Stories — are they supported?

Single videos, Reels, and Pins work when public links are used. Full playlist/story batch saves may be limited by the platform or link type.

Can I download private or age-restricted content?

No. Only publicly available content is supported. We cannot bypass paywalls, private links, DMs, or age checks.

Is it safe? What data do you store?

We don’t require accounts or store your history. Processing is automated. Avoid sharing sensitive links; always use public URLs you trust.

I get an error. What should I check?

Ensure the link is public and copied correctly. Platform rate-limits, geo-blocks, or removed videos can cause failures. Try again later or another link.

Is it legal to download videos?

Only download content you own or have permission to use. Respect each platform’s Terms and local copyright laws.

Note: Public links work best. Private links, deleted posts, or region-locked videos may not be downloadable.
